Muchao is currently a Ph.D. student at the [Instituto de Telecomunicações (IT)](https://www.it.pt/Members/Index/40316)/[Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (DEEC), Instituto Superior Técnico (IST)](https://tecnico.ulisboa.pt/en/), [University of Lisbon (ULisboa)](https://www.ulisboa.pt/en).
He graduated from the School of Mechanical Engineering of Sichuan University, earning a B.S. and M.S. degree in Mechanical Engineering. His research interests include but are not limited to Structural Health Monitoring, Radio Frequency Identification-based Sensor, as well as Instrumentation and Measurement.
