## Wavenumber and wavelength

The unit of wavenumber is $ rad/mm $ or $ rad/m $ . The unit of wavelength is $ mm $ or $ m $ . Assuming the wavelength is $ \alpha \ mm $, the wavenumber is $ \beta \ rad/mm $. Then,

$$ 
\alpha \cdot \beta = 2\pi
$$
